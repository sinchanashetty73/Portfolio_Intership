FoodSphere AI – Smart Food Donation, Restaurant Booking & Night Deals Platform

FoodSphere AI is a full-stack web application designed to reduce food waste, support food donations, and provide affordable meals through AI-powered recommendations and real-time notifications. The platform connects restaurants, donors, NGOs, event organizers, and food seekers in a single ecosystem.
